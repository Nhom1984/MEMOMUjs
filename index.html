MEMOMU - Memory & Music Game

Game Instructions:

  • Music Memory: Listen to the melody and repeat the sequence by clicking the colored tiles
  • Memory Classic: Find matching pairs by flipping cards
  • MEMOMU Memory: Watch the flashing sequence and click tiles in the correct order
  • Monluck: Find all the hidden monad symbols
  • Battle: Choose your fighter and compete against AI in memory challenges

Setup Instructions:

  • Place your images (MEMOMU1.png, monad.png, image1.png - image33.png) in the /assets/ folder
  • Place your sounds (yupi.mp3, kuku.mp3, buuuu.mp3, MEMOMU.mp3) in the /assets/ folder
  • Open this index.html file in your browser to play
